You will see {{ num_movies }} fictional movie concepts (a poster + short synopsis for each). For each movie, your task is to:

  1. Judge the movie yourself on two things:
    • Whether the movie would be more enjoyed by the right vs. the left.
    • Whether the movie's overall "vibe" feels right-leaning vs. left-leaning.
  2. Predict what most other participants will choose for those same two questions.

So, on each movie page you will answer four questions:

  1. Your judgment: which political group is more likely to enjoy the movie.
  2. Your prediction: what most other participants will choose for that question.
  3. Your judgment: the political "vibe" of the movie concept.
  4. Your prediction: what most other participants will choose for that vibe question.

You may also complete a short exit questionnaire after the last movie.
