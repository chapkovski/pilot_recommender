You will see 16 fictional movies (a poster and a short synopsis for each).

Your task

For each movie, you will answer two questions about the movie's overall vibe suggested by the poster and synopsis.

Each time the scale will be:

  • 1 = clearly left/progressive-leaning vibe
  • 4 = neutral / unclear
  • 7 = clearly right/conservative-leaning vibe

A 1 or 7 does not mean the movie is explicitly "about politics." It only reflects the overall vibe the movie gives off. If you don't notice a clear direction, choose 4. That's a useful answer.

Question 1 - Your judgment

What's the overall vibe of this movie? (1-7)

Question 2 - Other participants' judgments

What do you think most other participants will answer for Question 1 for this movie? (1-7)

"Other participants" are other people who complete this same study.

Duration and payment

  • Estimated duration: ~30 minutes
  • Base payment: €1.50

Additional bonus: You can earn an additional bonus based on how close your guess is to what most other participants answered. For each movie, you earn €0.10 if your guess in Question 2 is within 1 point on the 1-7 scale; otherwise you earn no extra money.

You never lose money.

Final questions

After the last movie, you will answer a few short final questions.
