Study title: Recommender Systems as Public Good

This study examines how people predict the average perception of political leaning and audience appeal for fictional movie concepts. You will see movie posters and short synopses, then answer prediction questions.

Researchers: Caterina Giannetti (University of Pisa), Philipp Chapkovski (University of Duisburg-Essen).

Contact: caterina.giannetti@unipi.it

Data protection contact (University of Pisa): responsabileprotezione-dati@unipi.it

Participation is voluntary. You may stop at any time without penalty.

We collect your study responses, basic demographics, political self-placement, and Prolific Participant ID (for administration). We do not ask for direct personal identifiers like name or phone number.

Data are processed for scientific research based on your consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)). Since political self-placement may reveal political opinions, explicit consent also applies (GDPR Art. 9(2)(a)).

Data are safeguarded with restricted access and stored separately for administration vs research analysis. You may withdraw consent before full anonymization.
